Joji

The once infamous YouTube sensation, "Filthy Frank", now a up and rising figure in the R&B/Hiphop charts, George Miller, a.k.a Joji, is one of the label's biggest hits. His debut album "Ballads I" cracked the Top 100 charts on Billboard's R&B records, and in doing so, he became the first ever Asian born artist to achieve that feat. From being the person behind the "Harlem Shake" to producing hits like "Slow Dancing in The Dark", there's no telling what Joji would be upto next.
