Rich Brian

Brian Imanuel, a.k.a Rich Brian, grew up in Indonesia and learnt English through YouTube videos, especially rap music. He worked on several vines and comedy sketches, until he released his first rap song, "Dat $tick". The video went viral, and caught the attention of several veteran rappers like Ghostface Killah. He then went on to sign with 88Rising, and went on to become a sensation back home in Indonesia and elsewhere.
